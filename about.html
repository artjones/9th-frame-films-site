About

I don’t make content.
I make films.

9th Frame Films is a small, independent studio led by Anthony Jones — a filmmaker, cinematographer, and screenwriter focused on story-driven, cinematic work rooted in real people and lived experience.

Philosophy

Quiet. Intentional. Human.

Story first

Every project starts with people, not gear. The goal isn’t coverage or “content” — it’s meaning. Images should feel lived-in, not performed.

Cinematic by design

Natural light. Controlled movement. Clean color. Simple setups that let moments breathe. Film language over trends.

Small crew energy

Most productions are solo or minimal by choice. Smaller sets feel calmer, more honest, and less intrusive — which leads to better performances and real reactions.

Independent spirit

Built outside of agencies and production houses. Direct collaboration. Fewer layers. Clear intent. The work stays personal.
